"We were not the first, but I am sure we will not be the last. After

us will come many other countries, driven, ladies and gentlemen, by

two unstoppable forces: freedom and equality. We are not legislating,

ladies and gentlemen, for remote unknown people. We are expanding

opportunities for the happiness of our neighbours, our work

colleagues, our friends, our relatives."
