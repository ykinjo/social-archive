# [mixi] Michelle Obama@Howard University

**作成日:** 2009-02-20

学生に向けたことば、だそうな。



“The question that I hate most that we ask of young people is, ‘What are you going to be when you grow up?’ And the truth is, I still don’t know, and I’m 45 years old,” she said. （若い人に対する質問で私が一番嫌いなのは、将来何になる？という質問。正直なところ、私もまだわからない、45歳になっても。）



ニュースでちらっとみて、探してみました。器の大きい人って感じですねえ。

続きはこんなの。「自分らしく」ってことですね。



“All I know is that it’s important for you to be true to yourselves, not to worry too much about what other people are going to think or make of your choices, because everyone will question what you do and tell you you should’ve done it the other way.”
